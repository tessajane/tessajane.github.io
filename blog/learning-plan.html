My long-term goal and or career pathway

Ideally I would like to find employment as a junior web developer when I finish the EDA course. I'm new to IT but I have always been interested in having a career in this industry because it's fast-paced and requires a lot of problem-solving.

Skills I want to develop at EDA?

Mostly technical skills. However, I am always open to learning new things and I'm looking forward to the 'core skills' part of the course. I am also enjoying the weekly sprint reflections more than I thought I would because it helps to regularly reflect on achievements and setbacks.
